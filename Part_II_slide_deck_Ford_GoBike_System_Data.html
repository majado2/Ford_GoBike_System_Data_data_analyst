Dataset Overview¶

The dataset consists of bicycle trip records (shared from the Ford GoBike System Data) and is described as follows:
It contains trip information, the time of the trip, the time and date of the start of the trip, the station information, the type of users, the gender of the users, the age of the users, as well as the participation of the users of the information and the bike number.
